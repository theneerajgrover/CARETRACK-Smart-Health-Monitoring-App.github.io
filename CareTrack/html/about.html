About CareTrack

AI Powered Symptom Checker for Smarter Health Decisions

What is CareTrack?

CareTrack is an intelligent symptom analysis web application that helps users understand possible health conditions by analyzing the symptoms they enter. The system provides guidance that can help users make informed health decisions.

How It Works

1️⃣ Enter Symptoms

Select symptoms you are experiencing.

2️⃣ AI Analysis

The system evaluates possible conditions.

3️⃣ Health Insights

Users receive possible explanations.

4️⃣ Next Steps

Guidance on when to consult a doctor.

Why Use CareTrack?

CareTrack helps people quickly understand their symptoms and prepare for medical consultations. It saves time and improves awareness about health conditions before visiting a healthcare professional.

Medical Disclaimer

CareTrack provides informational guidance only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified doctor for diagnosis and treatment.
