Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about CareTrack

Is CareTrack a medical diagnosis tool? ▼
CareTrack provides health insights based on symptoms but it does not replace professional medical diagnosis.
How does CareTrack analyze symptoms? ▼
The system compares the symptoms entered by the user with a medical knowledge database to identify possible related conditions.
Is my health data stored? ▼
CareTrack prioritizes user privacy and does not permanently store personal health data.
When should I consult a doctor? ▼
If symptoms are severe, persistent, or worsening, you should immediately consult a qualified medical professional.
How accurate are the results? ▼
CareTrack provides possible health insights based on symptom patterns, but results should always be verified with a doctor.
