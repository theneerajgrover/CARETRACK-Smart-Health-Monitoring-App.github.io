CareTrack

“Check today. Protect tomorrow.”

Overview

CareTrack is a smart health monitoring application designed to help users perform basic health checkups, generate quick health reports, and access emergency assistance when needed. The app focuses on preventive healthcare by allowing users to track essential health information and receive helpful insights.

Problem Statement

Many people ignore early health symptoms or do not have quick access to health monitoring tools. During emergencies, sharing health information with medical services can also be slow. CareTrack aims to simplify health tracking and enable faster response during critical situations.

Solution

CareTrack provides a simple platform where users can store their personal health information, monitor health parameters, generate reports, and automatically share emergency details with nearby NGOs or government hospitals when necessary.

Key Features

User Profile Management – Users can enter and save their personal and health details.

Health Monitoring – Track basic health parameters.

Automatic Report Generation – Generate quick health reports based on entered data.

Emergency SOS Feature – Automatically send the user’s health details and report to nearby NGOs or government hospitals during emergencies.

Simple User Interface – Easy-to-use design for quick access to health information.


How It Works

1. The user creates a profile and enters basic health information.


2. The app stores the data securely.


3. Users can update their health parameters anytime.


4. The system generates a health report.


5. In case of emergency, the SOS feature sends the user's details and report to nearby medical assistance providers.
